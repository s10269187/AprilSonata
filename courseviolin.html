Open Strings and Basic Bowing Beginners will learn the four open strings (G, D, A, and E) and how to read notes in the treble clef. They will play simple tunes and practise basic rhythms like crotchets, minims, and semibreves. Bowing techniques such as détaché (separate bows) and legato (smooth bowing) will be introduced.
