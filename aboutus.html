Who Are We?

At April Sonata, we believe that the beauty of music should never come at the cost of nature. That is why we craft our musical instruments using sustainably sourced materials, ensuring a low carbon footprint while preserving the planet's resources. We stand firmly against animal cruelty, replacing traditional materials like ivory with ethical, eco-friendly alternatives. Every instrument you purchase contributes to saving wildlife, reducing deforestation, and promoting a greener future, so you can play with purpose and harmony!
